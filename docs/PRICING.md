## FAQ

**Isn't it MIT — why would I pay?** You're not paying for the code. You're paying
for hosted compute, save/share, big exports, curriculum, and support — the stuff
a fork doesn't give you.

**Will the free tier get worse to push upgrades?** No. The free browser explorer
and local desktop app stay fully functional forever. We only meter *cloud* and
*production export*.

**Can I cancel anytime?** Yes — monthly, no contract. Annual is refundable
pro-rata in the first 30 days.

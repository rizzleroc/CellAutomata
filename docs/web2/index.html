III   M A R G I N A L I A

Four automata share one canvas — Conway's Life (1970), Wolfram's elementary 1D rules (2002), Pearson's Gray–Scott reaction–diffusion (1993), and a Brownian primordial soup. Pick a rule. Paint with the mouse. The link in COPY LINK serialises the rule and its parameters into the URL, so you can hand a coworker the exact frame you're looking at.
